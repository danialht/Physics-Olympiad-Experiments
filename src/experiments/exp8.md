## Harmonic Motion
The equation of motion for a harmonic oscillator:

$$\frac{d^2x}{dt^2} + \omega^2 x = 0$$

Solution:
$$x(t) = A \cos(\omega t + \phi)$$

Where:
- **A** is amplitude
- **ω** is angular frequency
- **φ** is phase constant

## Energy in Harmonic Motion
Total mechanical energy:

$$E = \frac{1}{2}m\omega^2 A^2$$
